Snippets and conditions are used for single-sourcing (also known as content reuse).

Where can you store snippet files in Flare?

Anywhere in the Resources folder in the Content Explorer

In the Resources > Snippets folder in the Content Explorer

In a custom folder in the Content Explorer

In the Project Organizer

You can store snippets pretty much anywhere you want in the Content Explorer, although we recommend you keep them in the Resources > Snippets folder.

Where can you store condition tags in Flare?

Anywhere in the Content Explorer

Anywhere in the Project Organizer

In the Conditions folder in the Project Organizer

In the XML Editor

Conditions are stored only in the Conditions folder, which is located in the Project Organizer.

Where can conditions be applied in Flare?

Files, such as topics

Content within topics

Entries within a table of contents (TOC)

eLearning ribbon

You can apply conditions to files, content, and entries in a TOC.
