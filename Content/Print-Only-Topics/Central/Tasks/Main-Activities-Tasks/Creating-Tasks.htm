Creating and Assigning Tasks

How to Create a Task

  1. On the left side of the interface, click Tasks.
  2. In the toolbar click .

  3. At the top, on the Details tab, enter a title for the task.

  4. (Optional) Complete any of the other fields in the dialog:

  5. (Optional) At the top of the dialog, select Discussion. You can use this area to add comments and maintain a conversation with others about the task.

    You can also use the paper clip button to add attachments to a comment.

  6. (Optional) At the top of the dialog, select Attachments. You can use this tab to associate one or more files with the task. Others can then click the ellipsis next to an attachment to download it.

  7. Click Save.
